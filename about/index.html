About

Jintian (Wayne) Wang

I'm an MPhys physics student at the University of Oxford (Exeter College), originally from Shanghai. I like building things end to end — from solving the differential equations, to writing the code, to soldering the hardware that makes it move.

My work sits at the intersection of physics, computation, and interaction: VR hand-tracking systems, transformers built from scratch, fine-tuned language models, and a DIY 3D printer. This site collects those projects along with notes and writing.
